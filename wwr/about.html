History

Back in 1572, Grandpa Joe took his wooden canoe down a white water rapid, he later found himself in the ocean stranded. Then multiple years of living off of fish, birds, and rain. He hit land, he was the secret first immigrant to the Americas.

He then taught all of the natives about river rafting, but unfortunately he died in a rafting accident going down what is now known as Niagara Falls. But the natives then taught the pilgrims about rafting and then the Old Joe Rafting Co was formed.
